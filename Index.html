Dear students and colleagues:
I thought of creating this website (as an online IREX portfolio) for you to be able to have an idea about the important events and contents of my visit to The United States of America as a participant in the International Leadership in Education Program (ILEP).
The program has been  funded by the Department of State, administered by International Research and Exchange Board (IREX) , in cooperation with Binational Fulbright   commission in Cairo, Egypt, and took place at Kent State University in Kent, Ohio.
It has been an interesting experience, full of many useful things to learn. I am very lucky to have been mentored by Dr. William Wilen, who is of great support to me.
I was also welcomed to attend two great classes to study about Distance Education and Current Issues in Educational Technology, administered by Dr. Drew Tiene.
My internship took place in Lakewood High School where I was supported and guided by a great ESL teacher, Mrs. Rebecca Havel.
Please feel free to browse my website for information about My IREX Portfolio ...                                Adel Henein
